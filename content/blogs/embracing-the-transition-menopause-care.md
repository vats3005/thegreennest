Menopause is a natural milestone in a woman's life, marking the end of the reproductive years. While it is a completely normal biological process, the physical and emotional shifts it brings can sometimes feel overwhelming. At The Green Nest, we believe this phase should be managed with expert care and deep understanding.

## Recognizing the Signs

The transition into menopause, known as perimenopause, can start years before your final period. During this time, fluctuating estrogen levels can lead to several common symptoms:

- Irregular menstrual cycles
- Hot flashes and night sweats
- Sleep disturbances and fatigue
- Mood shifts or increased anxiety
- Changes in bone density

## Personalized Management Strategies

Every woman experiences menopause differently, which means your care plan must be entirely unique to you. Dr. Shilpi Srivastava focuses on comprehensive symptom management to protect your long term health and improve your daily quality of life.

- **Hormone Therapy:** For many women, customized hormone replacement can safely and effectively relieve severe hot flashes and prevent bone loss.
- **Alternative Medications:** If hormone therapy is not right for you, we offer alternative medical treatments to target specific symptoms like mood changes or sleep issues.
- **Bone Health Focus:** The drop in estrogen significantly increases the risk of osteoporosis. We prioritize bone density screenings and calcium management.
- **Lifestyle Adjustments:** We provide targeted guidance on nutrition, exercise routines, and stress reduction techniques that naturally support your body through this transition.
